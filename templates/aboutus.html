About Us

In this proposed system, we aim to balance the asymmetry in communication which is needed to promote information flow between blind and non-blind individuals. Our project facilitates assistance to sighted individuals in reading and understanding documents of Braille cells. A deep learning-based recognition system is presented which is used to translate Braille images into multilingual text.
